Ultima White FAQs – Solihull

WHAT DENTAL AND AESTHETIC SERVICES DO YOU OFFER?

We provide a full range of services such as teeth whitening, veneers, Invisalign, Botox, dermal fillers, laser hair removal and the latest skin rejuvenation treatments.

WHAT TREATMENT IS BEST FOR ME?

We offer free consultations with our professional dental and aesthetic team to help determine which treatment is right for you and your individual goals.

HOW LONG DOES A TEETH WHITENING TREATMENT TAKE?.

Depending on the procedure, our whitening treatments last from 30 minutes to one hour.

HOW LONG DOES WHITENING LAST ON YOUR TEETH?

Results can remain anywhere from six month up to two years depending on your lifestyle and oral care routine.

HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE TO GET VENEERS?

Normally, the veneering process takes two or three visits to the dentist separated by a few weeks.

HOW LONG DO VENEERS LAST?

When they are cared for, veneers can prove to be long lasting, keeping their natural look as well as their function.

HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE FOR INVISALIGN TO WORK?

Invisalign Treatments are usually concluded between 12 to 18 months depending on your level of dental alignment.

IS INVISALIGN TREATMENT UNCOMFORTABLE?

Invisalign is almost completely painless (teeth can feel a little preview sore when first coming on board), apart from a brief period when you first wear them.

We do not operate with high overheads like a typical dental office, so we can pass our savings on to you.

HOW MANY SHADES WHITER WILL MY TEETH BE?

Results vary, though we’ve yet to come across anyone whose teeth didn’t lighten by at least four shades—many clients see improvements of up to 10 or 12 shades.
